California & San Francisco Compliance Requirements

California Energy Commission (CEC) AB 802

  • Applies to commercial and multifamily buildings over 50,000 sq ft
  • Annual energy benchmarking submission required
  • Energy use data collection and verification
  • Compliance tracking and reporting to state

San Francisco Existing Buildings Ordinance (EBO)

  • Applies to non-residential buildings with 10,000 sq ft or more of heated/cooled space
  • Applies to multifamily residential buildings with 50,000 sq ft or more of conditioned space
  • Annual energy benchmarking due May 1st via ENERGY STAR Portfolio Manager
  • Energy audits required every 5 years for non-residential buildings
  • Utility data aggregation and verification required

Help your high-performing building achieve ENERGY STAR® certification. We guide you through the process, verify performance data, and ensure your building meets EPA's strict standards for national recognition.

Audit Exemption: Buildings that maintain ENERGY STAR® certification for 3 consecutive years qualify for exemption from mandatory energy audits

Large commercial buildings in San Francisco must transition to 100% renewable electricity. Documentation must be submitted by April of the year following each deadline.
