Why Energy Compliance Matters

Build Awareness & Understanding

Energy benchmarking provides building owners with critical insights into their energy consumption patterns. Buildings that benchmark their energy use identify an average of 30% more efficiency opportunities compared to those that don't track their performance, enabling data-driven decisions that reduce waste and costs.

Reduce Energy Bills

Buildings that consistently benchmark and act on their data see measurable financial benefits. ENERGY STAR certified buildings use an average of 35% less energy and generate 35% fewer greenhouse gas emissions than typical buildings.

Protect the Environment

Energy benchmarking is a critical tool in the fight against climate change. Buildings account for approximately 37% of total U.S. energy consumption (including electrical system losses). By tracking and reducing energy use, we can significantly cut carbon emissions and contribute to a more sustainable future.

San Francisco & California Compliance Requirements

San Francisco Existing Buildings Ordinance (EBO)

Applies to non-residential buildings with 10,000 sq ft or more of heated/cooled space. Applies to multifamily residential buildings with 50,000 sq ft or more of conditioned space. Annual energy benchmarking due May 1st. Energy audits required every 5 years for non-residential buildings. Utility data aggregation and verification required.

California Energy Commission (CEC) AB 802

Applies to commercial and multifamily buildings over 50,000 sq ft. Annual energy benchmarking submission required. Compliance tracking and reporting to state.

Components of Compliance

Energy Benchmarking

Complete energy benchmarking services for CEC AB 802 and San Francisco EBO compliance. We collect utility data, calculate EUI (Energy Use Intensity), and submit reports on time to avoid penalties. San Francisco EBO Deadline: May 1st annually.

Energy Audits

Comprehensive energy assessments that identify waste and inefficiencies. Our audits go beyond compliance to deliver actionable recommendations that reduce costs and improve building performance. SF EBO Requirement: Every 5 years for non-residential buildings. ASHRAE Level I & II assessments available.

ENERGY STAR® Certification

Help your high-performing building achieve ENERGY STAR® certification. We guide you through the process, verify performance data, and ensure your building meets EPA's strict standards for national recognition. Buildings that maintain ENERGY STAR® certification for 3 of the 5 years prior to the audit due date qualify for exemption from mandatory energy audits in San Francisco.
